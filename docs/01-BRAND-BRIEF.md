# Brand Brief — Abu Irfan for Glass & Aluminum Co.

## Identity

**Abu Irfan for Glass & Aluminum Co.** is a Dubai-based specialist in the **design, fabrication, and installation** of glass and aluminum systems — for residential villas, commercial buildings, and retail/hospitality spaces across the UAE.

### Positioning statement
> *We craft precision glass and aluminum solutions that bring light, openness, and lasting value to every space — engineered for the Dubai climate, finished to a standard you can see and feel.*

### Brand personality (3 words)
**Precise · Premium · Trustworthy**

### What makes us different (to emphasize)
- **In-house design + fabrication + installation** — one accountable team, start to finish.
- **Built for the UAE climate** — thermal-break, double-glazing, heat- & dust-rated systems.
- **Custom, not catalogue** — every project sized and finished to the property.
- **Real craftsmanship & clean finishing** — the details that separate a good job from a great one.

## Tone of voice

| Do | Don't |
|---|---|
| Clear, confident, expert | Hype, buzzwords, keyword-stuffing |
| Concrete (specs, materials, process) | Vague aspirational filler |
| Respectful, consultative | Pushy sales speak |
| Short sentences, scannable | Long padded paragraphs |

**Example line (good):** *"Our thermal-break aluminum frames stop heat at the metal, so your room stays cool and your bills stay low — even in a Dubai summer."*

## Visual brand direction (summary — full detail in `05-DESIGN-BRIEF.md`)

- **Mood:** Architectural, calm, light-filled, engineered precision. The feeling of *glass and brushed metal*.
- **Keywords:** clarity, structure, light, craftsmanship, restraint.
- **Logo:** Request vector source (SVG/AI). Need full-color, all-white (for dark sections), and monochrome variants + favicon.
- **Photography:** Real projects only — sharp, bright, architectural. Avoid generic stock and the competitor's reused imagery.
