## 2. Aluminum & Glazing

### Aluminium Glass Works (hub)
- Full design–fabricate–install service: doors, partitions, curtain walls, thermal-break systems, glazing. Built for UAE heat/sand/UV; energy-efficient.
- **FAQ Qs:** What are aluminium glass works? / Why aluminium in Dubai? / What is thermal-break aluminium? / Is double-glazing suitable for Dubai? / Custom fabrication? / Cost?

### Glass Doors & Windows
- Design-feature doors/windows: natural light, security, durability, energy efficiency, custom fabrication.

### Glass Partition
- Office/workspace zoning that keeps light & openness; privacy + aesthetics. Variations: frameless, aluminum-framed, smart glass.

### Sliding / Folding / Telescopic / Stackable Doors
- **Sliding:** space-saving, panoramic, smooth-gliding. **Folding (bi-fold)/stackable:** wide clear openings, flexible indoor-outdoor. **Frameless folding:** unobstructed views, seamless flow. **Telescopic:** multi-panel, compact-stacking, space-saving wide openings (commercial + residential).

### Curtain Walls
- Non-structural aluminum-and-glass facade systems; architectural statement, max natural light, thermal performance, weather resistance.

### Glass Types
- **Laminated:** bonded interlayers, shatter-resistant, safety/security, UV & sound reduction. **Double-glazed:** two panes + sealed air gap → thermal & acoustic insulation, lower bills, year-round comfort. (Add **toughened/tempered** & **tinted/reflective**.)

### Smart Glass
- PDLC switchable glass — transparent ↔ opaque on demand for instant privacy; modern, eliminates blinds.

### ACP Cladding
- Aluminum Composite Panel facade cladding — durable, weather-protective, contemporary exterior aesthetic.

### Aluminium Systems (Thermal-Break & Dual-Color)
- **Thermal-break:** insulating barrier in the frame stops heat conduction → cooler interiors, lower energy use, ideal for UAE. **Dual-color:** powder-coated two colors (inside vs. outside face) for coordinated interior/exterior design.

### Staircase Glass
- Glass balustrades/panels for staircases & split-levels; striking focal point, light flow between floors, safety-rated.

### Fly & Mesh Screens
- **Fly screen:** insect protection + ventilation, integrated into doors/windows. **Pleated mesh:** retractable, configurable (top-to-bottom, center-opening, single-panel).
