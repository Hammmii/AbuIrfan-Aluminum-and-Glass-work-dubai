# Abu Irfan for Glass & Aluminum Co — Website Project

> Single source of truth for the new website build. Read this first.

## 1. What we are building

A **premium, information-led marketing website** for **Abu Irfan for Glass & Aluminum Co.** — a Dubai-based glass & aluminum fabrication and installation company. The site must out-class the reference competitor (`elegantrooms.ae`) in every dimension: design, content depth, motion, performance, and credibility.

This is **not** an e-commerce site. Primary goals:
1. Establish Abu Irfan as a credible, premium, expert glass & aluminum specialist.
2. Generate qualified leads (quote requests, calls, WhatsApp).
3. Rank well locally for glass & aluminum services in Dubai/UAE.

## 5. Guiding principles (non-negotiable)

1. **Decent & premium, never generic.** No default Inter/Roboto, no Lucide/Font-Awesome emoji-style icons pasted into rounded squares, no Elementor "AI template" look.
2. **Unique typography pairing** — a distinctive display face + a clean text face (see `05-DESIGN-BRIEF.md`).
3. **Purposeful motion** — scroll-reveal, parallax on glass imagery, magnetic/hover micro-interactions. Motion explains the product (light, transparency, precision), never decorates for its own sake.
4. **Content depth > keyword stuffing.** Every service page gets a real definition, scope, materials, process, use cases, and FAQs.
5. **Performance & accessibility** — fast, responsive, mobile-first, semantic, Lighthouse 90+.
